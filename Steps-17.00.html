Monthly Data Talent Job Fair 17

Every month on the Friday after the last Thursday of the month, we will hold a Virtual Data Talent Job Fair.  Employers will come to this event with intentions on interviewing and hiring candidates.  You will be assigned a room where an employer can look at your profile and decide to come to your room where they can interview you on the spot.  This service is available to you not only while you are going through the training, but also when you become an alumni and ready to hit the market again.
