CAP Competition & Data Talent Showcase 14

The 1st Wed of every month, we will hold a CAP Competition.  This is a communication skills challenge where 5 students from either Data Analytics or Data Science classes will present their CAP project.  This is an event for the students where they will vote and the top 3 move on to the Data Talent Showcase on the last Thursday of the month.  This event will show up on your event calendar if you want to see what type of skills you will have as you get to the end of the program, this is the event you want to attend.



Top 3 winners of the CAP competition will perform in the Data Talent Showcase where we will bring hiring managers and experts in the field to help cirtique your project presentations.
