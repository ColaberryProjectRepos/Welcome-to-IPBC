Center of Excellence 9


To be ready for the field, there are many lessons you need learn .As you get ready for the field, you can choose to learn all the lessons yourself (slower) or you can use existing data to learn from others (faster) in the Network. Each CAP program has a Center of Excellence filled with Students and Alumni.  The COE is used to take the expeirence of the group and share it with the entire Network for faster learning.  This is also the Project Managment Tool used to build all the projects.  You will recieve emails from other projects which is designed to expose you to many project and help you gain expeirence much faster than you would on your own.
