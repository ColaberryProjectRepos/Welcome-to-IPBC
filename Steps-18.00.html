IPBC Pay Options 18

IPBC is $15k.  If you pay up front, it's $12k.  When you 1st signup for IPBC you will automatically be signed up for payment option 1 and your account is billed based on payment option 1.  Below are the payment option details.  If you choose payment option 2 then you won't have to pay the currently billed amount.



Payment Option 1

  • $250 per Mth - payment switches to $100 per Mth when you get to the CAP program.
  • Colaberry invests in your education.
  • Once you get placed or quit /no longer active in our system $15k - IPBC Fees paid.
  • 1st month payment is prorated. For example if you came on the 15th, you'd be billed $125 1st month.





Payment Option 2

  • Sign a contract with Mentorworks
  • They fund your education so you don't pay Colaberry anymore
  • You have 12 months to start paying them back (more than enough time to finish the program)
  • Citizen or GC holder start for free.
  • For Non Citizen or GC holder, there is a 1 time $500 fee.  This is the cost we incur for Mentorworks to manage your ISA.


DETAILS

We have already sent you an email with the details of the ISA. Email support@colaberry.com if you don't have it. Check your spam as it could be there as well.  On that email is all the details of the ISA along with how to signup. 


DISCOUNT

If you sign up less than one month after starting IPBC, we are offering discount that would cover the Mentorworks fees ($2499).  The discount is automatic and goes away after your 1st month is passed.
