Colaberry Chat App 6


By now you should be familiar with Colaberry Chat App.  This is the 1st place you should go (besides google) for help.  We use the Colaberry Network to help everyone grop.  Below are instructions on how to post the 3 different question types.


Congratulations!  You have been added to a group. This template will guide you on how to post questions including questions that need answers, informational questions, and schedule calls.


To get the gif version of this document, click on the red button above this step.

 

Answer Needed – Choose this option when you need help and are looking for response.  Be sure to add a screenshot, detailed explanation and what you have tried.  These 3 things will help others better understand your exact problem which puts everyone in a better position to help you. You will receive an email to notify you when you get a response.






Informational Post – When submitting a Colaberry Chat assignment or you just want to help educate the group and don’t require a response, use this option. Always cite your sources to avoid plagiarism when you make an informational post.






Schedule a Call – This is what you will use to schedule your group calls.  You can schedule a call for a date/time in the future and group members will have the ability to subscribe to the call.  All subscribers will be emailed minutes before the call, or the video call can be accessed by navigating directly to the group.  Upon completion of the call, the video call will be downloaded and posted in the chat automatically where you can review later.  Use this option for your weekly calls and create them in your Colaberry Chat group created for your group.
