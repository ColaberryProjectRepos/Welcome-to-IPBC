Class IPBC Mentor 4


You will also be assigned a Class mentor automatically based on when you signed up for IPBC.  While your IPBC mentor will have other mentees at various stages of the program, your Class Mentor will have more students that joined IPBC in the same month you joined.  Your mentor is responsible for guiding you through IPBC.  They will be instrumental in giving you help and guiding you in the right direction when you are stuck.  Your Class Mentor will..

  • Have classes once per week
  • Colaberry Chat group
  • Provide heavy guidance via Colaberry Chat

Mentors have been through the program and are currently placed in the field.  They know what it takes to be successful in this program and their guidance is instrumental to your success.


Colaberry Chat Group

Once you are assigned a Class mentor, you will be automatically assigned to the Class Chat group. Use this group to communicate with your Class Mentor as well as other students in the Class group.

Format of group Name: DA_IPBC_2022_Mth
