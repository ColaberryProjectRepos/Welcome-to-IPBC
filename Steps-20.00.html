Overview:

By 2023, the only two options of achieving education through Colaberry will be paying upfront or obtaining an ISA.



Payment Option 1:

Payment Option 2:

  • Sign a contract with Mentorworks

DETAILS

If you have already signed up for IPBC - We have already sent you an email with the details of the ISA. Check your spam as it could be there as well.  On that email is your personalized numbers  of the ISA along with how to signup. 


DISCOUNT

If you sign up less than one month after starting IPBC, we are offering discount that would cover the Mentorworks fees ($2499).  The discount is automatic and goes away after your 1st month is passed. This discount will go away at the end of 2022.



Payment Option 3:


This payment option will ONLY be available until the end of 2022. Whether you are placed or not, you will need to migrate to an ISA by 2023.

  • $250 per mth ACH payment until you get placed.
    • Current Students that choose this option will need to fill out the above attachment  and submit to ali@colaberry.com
