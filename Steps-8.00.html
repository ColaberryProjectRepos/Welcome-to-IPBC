Mock Interviews 8

Throughout the program you will practice with Mock Interviews designed to make you more comfortable while conducting interviews.


Automated Mock Interviews - This is an automated system where you can pick and schedule specific interviews and once schedule, the system will call you and record, as the question and record the session you can review later.


Instructor Mock Interviews - Our Instructors and mentors will be on call when you need instructor mock interviews to prepare for the real interviews.  When you get closer to placement you will conduct interviews with various instructions to help prepare you for the real thing.
