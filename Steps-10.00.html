Project Building 10


When you enter the Career Accelerator Program (CAP) towards the end of IPBC, as an intern your job will be to build a project from your chosen dataset and get it approved for Colaberry Network.  This project will also be used for experience to showcase to the employers in route to placement.  This project process will give you real world expeirence and make you more prepared to secure a Mid to Senior Level position.


View the project below to see all the things needed for a successful project including a video that tells that the story.
